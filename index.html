How to Buy tokens

• First of all, install MetaMask here and put some Ether in it.

• To buy ARW, simply click the "Buy Tokens" button and enter the amount of Ether you want to convert to ARW, based on the current Buy Price. Buying coins will increase both the Buy Price and Sell Price with 0.0000001 ETH per bought coin once the transaction is complete.

• When cashing out coins, the Buy Price and Sell Price drop afterwards, decreasing the value of your (and everyone else's) coins by 0.0000001 ETH per coin.

• You can move your tokens into Dividends which then is stored as Ether. To do this click on the "Sell Tokens" button - and your tokens will be transferred into Ether based upon the current sell price.

• You can also use your dividends to immediately purchase more ARW tokens by selecting "Buy with Dividends".

• To cash out your ARW, click the "Withdraw" button and follow along.
