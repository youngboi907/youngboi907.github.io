Overlay. Colored columns behind the candles are the live order-book depth ratio in the 1–2.5 / 2.5–5 / 5–10% bands around price. Spot shows green (bids dominant) / red (asks dominant); Futures shows cyan / amber. Toggle each independently. Brighter = stronger imbalance. The three panels plot each band's bid vs ask (toggle OBD depth ↔ RATIO, and RAW ↔ CUMulative). Top 10 marks the ten biggest resting bid/ask levels on the right of the chart. Drag to pan, scroll/pinch to zoom, drag the price axis to rescale. Data is recorded continuously to your browser (per symbol+market) and reloads when you reopen.
Run it: open in a normal browser tab — it needs network access to Binance and won't connect inside a chat preview.
